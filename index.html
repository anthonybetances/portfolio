About

I focused my studies on Crisis Management and Conflict Resolution, and applied these skills at Apple and later ICM Partners, where I assisted a talent agent in booking and servicing tours and festival plays for acts such as Migos, J. After exiting from entertainment, I shifted back into tech and joined Resilient Coders as a software engineer. Managing high-stakes projects and relationships in high-volume operations has forged me into a proactive problem-solver and a resourceful teammate. What more could you want in an engineer?
